Camp Fire


At Vimal resort, the campfire area is a magical place where guests gather under the starry night sky to bask in the warmth and glow of the flickering flames. The ambiance of a campfire is often considered to be peaceful and relaxing, as the crackling and popping sounds of the fire create a calming and comforting atmosphere. It's a great way to connect with nature at Vimal resort and spend time with friends and family in an outdoor setting.
As the night wears on, the fire slowly burns down to embers, signalling that it's time to say goodnight. But the memories made around the campfire at vimal resort will last a lifetime, creating a sense of warmth and camaraderie that will stay with guests long after they've returned home.
